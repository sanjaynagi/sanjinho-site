Inspired by a February visit to Florence and it's rich history of renaissance art, I recently developed a Python package, [*Tempera*](https://sanjaynagi.github.io/tempera/), that provides Python colour palettes for major Python plotting tools, extracted from major historic artworks. 

*Tempera* is an artistic medium made by mixing pigments with a water-soluble emulsion, often made of water and egg yolk. It was one of the primary painting techniques of the pre and early Renaissance periods — Botticelli's [The Birth of Venus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Birth_of_Venus), for instance, is egg tempera on canvas.
